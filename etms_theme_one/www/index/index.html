Wastewater Treatment Plant (Sewage and Industrial Solutions).




Industrial : We propose, design, manufacture and service the wastewater treatment plants for industries.
By selecting a suitable technology, we guarantee a stable and economical process of treatment of various types of waste water according to the nature of the industrial production.

Sewage: We are specialised in design, engineering, supply, installation & commissioning of sewage Treatment plants (STP) based on various technologies We offer you the latest and most appropriate technology available in treating sewage and waste water, whether for compliance with local regulations or for reuse. Every Sewage treatment plant is designed to suit our client's specific needs. We are able to offer varying degrees of treatment, depending upon operational requirements.
Containerized Solutions To Treat Water Ebkar Co. offers containerized wastewater treatment plants for easy setup and relocation. This solution is ideal for remote locations such as mining camps and oil rig sites.

We offer a variety of capacities that can meet your requirements.
Handy containerized wastewater treatment plant As the pre-assembled containerized wastewater treatment plant is located inside a standard 20 or 40 foot shipping container, it can be relocated and installed with ease at other locations. In most cases it is a one to two-day operation and requires only a flatbed truck and a crane.
The advantages of using containerized wastewater treatment plants are:
The systems are fully transportable and can easily be relocated and reinstalled at new sites. The site installation can be completed within one day with “plug & Play” systems. Supplied in different versions of integration of cleaning steps, transportability and capacity.

Water Storage Tanks



Our extensive range of water storage tanks includes concrete water tanks, above and underground water tanks, GRP water storage tanks, open top water tanks, food grade water tanks and coated steel tanks. We also supply potable (suitable for drinking water) tanks and non-potable water tanks.

All our tanks are manufactured from high-quality durable materials and vary rang of capacities ranging from 4 litres to 1,000,000 litres. This makes them suitable for a wide variety of purposes and situations such as rainwater harvesting, car valeting, window cleaning, wastewater treatment, chemical manufacturing, fire suppression, and agricultural, commercial, domestic and industrial water storage.

Pumping Systems



EEIS offers a complete range of pumps for water treatment, wastewater treatment, water pump stations, water wells and fire fighting systems such as booster pumps, centrifugal pumps, sewage pumps, sump pumps, metering mumps, chemical dosing pumps and all its Accessories.

Our pumps are used for various applications such as pressure boosting, ultrapure water systems, deionized systems, distribution systems, pre-filter charge systems, condensation systems, car wash, cooling towers, sprinkler and irrigation, parts washers, poultry / beverage processing, water reclamation, jocky pump, liquid transfer, heat exchangers, pharmaceutical services, spray systems, scrubbers, general pump applications and etc.
